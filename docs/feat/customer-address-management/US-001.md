## Title

[Customer Address Management] Add user story and scenarios for managing personal address book

---

## User Story

As a customer,
I want to create, view, update, and delete my saved addresses,
So that I can maintain an accurate and up-to-date address book for my orders.

---

## Scenario 1: Successful flow

Given I am logged in as a customer with an existing address book
When I navigate to my address management page
Then I see a list of my saved addresses with options to add, edit, or delete each one

---

## Scenario 2: Validation / error flow

Given I am adding a new address
When I submit the address form with missing required fields (street, city, state, postal code, or country)
Then the system displays validation errors and prevents the address from being saved
And I remain on the form to correct the errors

---

## Scenario 3: Alternate flow

Given I have a saved address
When I update the address details and save
Then the updated information is stored and reflected in my address list
And any orders that reference this address (if allowed by business rules) maintain the historical snapshot

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- The customer can view a list of all their saved addresses with complete details for each.
- The customer can add a new address by providing all required fields: street address, city, state/province, postal code, country.
- The customer can edit any of their saved addresses at any time.
- The customer can delete any of their saved addresses (subject to default address constraints).
- The system validates all required fields before accepting create or update operations.
- The system enforces ownership: a customer can only access their own addresses.
- The customer receives clear error messages when validation fails.
- The customer cannot edit or delete addresses belonging to other customers.
